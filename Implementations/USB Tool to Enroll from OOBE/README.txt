====================================================
                 Enroll From OOBE
====================================================

If you have a computer in OOBE that needs to be enrolled in Autopilot, download the Enroll in OOBE.zip and extract everything from the Paste all 3 folders into the root directory of your flashdrive folder to the root of your flashdrive. 

====================================================
                    How To Use 
====================================================

1. Plug in your USB device. 
2. Turn on the computer 
3. Press Shift + F10 to open CMD in OOBE 

4. Run: D:\AP.cmd (This may be E:\ but will most likely be D:\)

5. Follow the prompts and instructions in the PowerShell script
